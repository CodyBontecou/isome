Every visit and route, drawn together.

The map view stitches your significant locations and recorded paths into one calm picture. Tap a pin for visit details, scrub a route to replay it.

  • Color-coded markers for arrivals and departures
  • Route playback with distance & duration
  • Tap any visit to inspect & rename

▌ SETTINGS  ·  01

One panel. Total control.

Tracking modes, accuracy, auto-off timers, location naming — every knob you'd want, nothing you wouldn't. Designed for a one-tap setup, not a settings safari.

  • High-accuracy continuous mode for precise routes
  • Per-session auto-shutoff (1h, 2h, 4h, 8h)

Your data is yours. Export visits, points, or both — as JSON, CSV, or Markdown. Re-import them later, ship them to your own analytics, or just keep an offline backup.

  • Markdown export for personal journaling
